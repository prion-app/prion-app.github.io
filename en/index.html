This is Prion

A multi-media parent communication and documentation tool for preschools. In less than 30 seconds, teachers can document activities with photo, video and text, that parents can access on their mobile device.

  • Every child has a portfolio
  • Parents can follow their child
  • Reach out to parents quickly
